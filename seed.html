SEED

MVP Out in less than a Quarter

As successful entrepreneurs we know the value of time to market and market feedback. Seed is specifically designed to have a working viable product in a quarter. Staggering and Struggling products rarely make it to market because of a drop in focus. A high energy delivery in a quarter is what jump starts the product and brings in responsibility of catering to live customers.

Our team of business and technology experts help you with the process to capitalize market in lowest possible time.

The Seed product is explictly designed to allow you to delegate technology to our team so you may focus on business strategies.

This allows you to focus on what you know best vis. business.

How it works?

GET
SET
GO
Define Product vision, roadmap and delivery schedule insync with an an experienced architect.
Build a product with fortnightly deliverables to mark progress, with an expert team consisting of architects, UX developers, mobiler application designers and hard core server side experts
Go to market and launch the product

Benefits

Product Out in a Quarter allowing quick market capture and exposure to real world
Fortnightly Delivery marking progress, you see progress every fifteen days and don’t wait for end to see what is coming. This allows course corrections
Engagement with specialists who know what it takes to deliver products
Strong foundation laid out by experts with eon's of experience
Led by experienced architects to help manage scope and technologies
Team of experienced UX designers, devops, back end developers and mobile team working in synergy with your business
